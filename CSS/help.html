Community Help Center

Welcome to the official Local City Council Community Support Portal. We are committed to assisting residents in registering for local events, using civic services, and solving issues.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I register for an event? Simply navigate to the "Register" section on our main page, fill out the form, and click submit.
  • Are events free? Fees vary based on the event type. Selecting the event in the registration form will display the correct fee dynamically.
  • Can I clear my saved preferences? Yes, you can click "Clear Preferences" at the bottom of the registration form to clear stored data.
  • How does Geolocation work? Clicking "Find Nearby Events" requests access to your location to calculate the nearest community event space. We do not store your location data.

Troubleshooting Support

If you encounter layout or loading issues, we recommend checking if your browser has JavaScript enabled. If you see broken styles, verify that your network connection allows downloading our external stylesheet (styles.css).
